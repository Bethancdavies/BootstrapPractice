Research Skills and Publications

In Vivo

I have years of experience working with rats and mice- including breeding and maintaining transgenic lines. I regularly use preclinical models of stroke, cerebral palsy, and multiple sclerosis. I have lots of surgical experience, and am an expert in behavioural testing!

In Vitro

I have experience working with primary and immortalized cell cultures, growing organoids, performing neurosphere assays, and microfluidic cultures. I also have experience with in vitro injury models like oxygen-glucose deprivation.

Molecular Techniques

I use molecular techniques including protein and RNA analysis to determine regulation and expression of proteins, histology to investigate gross changes on a cellular and tissue level, and immunoflouresence to identify cells and proteins.
